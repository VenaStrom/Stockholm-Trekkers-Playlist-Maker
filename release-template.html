## Warning Installing versions 3.0.0 and above will remove previous save files so please back them up. On Windows, they can be found in `C:\Users\{username}\AppData\local\programs\stockholm-trekkers-playlist-maker\user-data\`. ## Installation 1. Click on the `.msi` file to download the installer. (`.deb` for Linux). - You may receive a warning in your browser. Click on `Keep` to continue. 2. Run the installer. - You may receive a warning from Windows.
